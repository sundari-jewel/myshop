C — Cinematic 50/50 Split

One unified section with a full-bleed split — watches occupies the left half, rakhi the right. Product images in the background, editorial copy over a fade-in overlay. Dramatic, high-impact, very modern luxury feel. One section instead of two keeps the page tight.
